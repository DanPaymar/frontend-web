About

Code Monkey

Nestled in the picturesque town of Fairfax, California, the code monkey's neighborhood is a vibrant blend of culinary delights and outdoor adventures. Grab a coffee from the Coffee Roastery, then embark on a thrilling mountain biking adventure along the scenic trails of Mount Tamalpais. After working up an appetite, unwind at the renowned Gestalt Haus, where you can savor locally brewed beers and mouthwatering pub fare. For dinner, indulge in izayaka and maki rolls at the chic Village Saki, where fresh ingredients are transformed into culinary masterpieces. Whether you're exploring the rugged beauty of Mount Tamalpais or savoring the flavors of Fairfax, this neighborhood offers a perfect balance of outdoor thrills and gastronomic pleasures.
